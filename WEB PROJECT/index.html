Fatima Handcrafts

Founded by potter Fatima Malik, her studio is rooted in Multan's centuries-old blue pottery tradition. Blending mineral dyes with handthrown forms, her work celebrates slow making and everyday beauty.

Zara Weaves

Founded by weaver Zara Hussain, her Faisalabad studio brings a fresh approach to traditional ajrak and khaddar fabric using century-old techniques passed down through generations.
